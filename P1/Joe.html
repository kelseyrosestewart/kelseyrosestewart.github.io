Joe Jonas

Instrument: Vocals, Guitar, and Piano

Zodiac Sign: Leo

Joe continued his music career after the break up, forming the band DNCE. You may be familiar with their hit song, “Cake by the Ocean”. Their later EP release, Sway, made the Billboard 200 chart, peaking at number nine.

Joe Jonas married Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner in 2019. They recently had a baby in July 2020.
